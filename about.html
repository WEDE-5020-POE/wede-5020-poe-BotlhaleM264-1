About Buddy's Comfort Kitchen

Established in 2019 by Buddy Smith, Buddy's Comfort Kitchen Catering has been serving delicious and comforting meals for all your catering needs. What started as a small family business has grown into a beloved catering service known for its mouthwatering dishes and exceptional customer service. Today, we employ 6 family members and 3 part-time staff, serving 160 weekly customers.

Our Mission

At Buddy's Comfort Kitchen, our mission is to provide delicious and comforting meals that bring people together. We believe that food has the power to create memorable experiences, and we are dedicated to making every event special with our diverse menu and exceptional service.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier catering service in the region, known for our commitment to quality, taste, and exceptional customer service.

Our Team

Our team of experienced chefs is passionate about creating comfort food that satisfies every palate. We use only the freshest ingredients to craft mouthwatering dishes that will make your event unforgettable. From classic comfort foods to innovative culinary creations, we offer a wide range of options to suit any occasion.
